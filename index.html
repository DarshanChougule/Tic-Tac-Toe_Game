Tic Tac Toe Game

Rules:

1.The goal is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of your symbols (either "X" or "O").
2.Players take turns to place their symbol (X or O) in an empty cell on the grid.
3.A player can place their symbol in any one of the empty spaces on their turn.
